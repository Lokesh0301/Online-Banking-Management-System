    Andhra University (AU) is one of the prestigious universities located in Visakhapatnam. In the recent placements, a total of 464 students were placed with salary packages ranging between 3 LPA to 13 LPA. With B.E/ B.tech and M.Sc as its popular courses, the university also runs various other programs in the streams of Arts, Science, and Technology, Commerce, Engineering, Management, Law, Pharmacy and Education. The selection for admission to the courses is entrance based.

    Andhra University Courses

    The University runs as many as 313 courses in Arts, Science, and Technology, Commerce, Engineering, Management, Law, Pharmacy and Education. It has five constituent colleges and four AU Campuses. The Colleges of Arts and Commerce is the biggest constituent college in the University with 26 Departments offering 42 courses including four Diploma Courses.

    Andhra University Distance Education

    AU SDE (Andhra University School of Distance Education) was started in 1972 by Andhra University. It has 23 permanent faculties. The school of distance education presently offering 4 UG Programs, 18 PG Programs, 5 Certification Courses, 9 PG Diploma Courses, 15 Professional Courses, and 7 Collaborative Courses through Distance Education Mode.

    Andhra University Results

    The University conducts Semester examination for its UG and PG courses under University teaching departments & affiliated colleges in the month of July every year. In order to check the course wise semester results, students have to follow the below-mentioned steps:

    1. Visit the official website of Andhra University.
    2. In the main menu, click on the ‘Examination’ tab.
    3. Now click on the results, and you will be redirected to the latest result page.
    4. After clicking on the desired link, a PDF will be displayed on the screen which displays the provisional (Roll Number wise) list of passed students.

    Ques. What are some special programmes offered by Andhra University?

    Ans. The University offers several courses in UG and PG. It also offers some special programmes in parasitology, marine fisheries, plant cytogenetics, hydrology, petroleum exploration studies, human genetics, nanosciences and entomology.

    Ques. What is the mode of admission at Andhra University?

    Ans. The mode of admission is online. Offline mode is not available for this college.

    Ques. How are the placements at Andhra University for B.Tech students?

    Ans. The B.Tech placements at Andhra University have a placement rate of 84-91% which is declining year-by-year. Earlier in 2016, the placement rate was 91%, which declined to 89% in 2017, and the recent placements of 2018 show 84% placement rate. The median salary offered to the students ranges between 3-4 LPA. One of the main reasons for this decline is the choice of students who opt for higher studies rather than participating in the placement process. In 2016, out of 375 students, 56 students opted for higher studies. In 2017, out of 355 students, 76 students chose a Master's degree for their next career option. And, in 2018 the no. continued to increase, out of 356 students 95 students opted for higher studies. The remaining few students who neither participate in On-campus placements nor they go for higher studies, find placements off-campus.

    Ques. Does Andhra University provide hostel accommodation to students?

    Ans. Yes, the university provides hostel accommodation to its students as per their school of study. There are a total of 8 hostels, namely:

    • Ladies Hostel, Maharanipeta
    • Law Hostel
    • Research Hostel
    • Engineering Ladies Hostel
    • International Students Hostel
